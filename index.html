About Me

Hi, I'm Elizabeth!

I'm a Data Analyst with a Master's degree in Physics. After breaking my leg in late 2022, I discovered my passion for data analytics and developed my skills during my recovery. As a former research scientist, I have a sharp eye for detail and a strong analytical mindset that I apply to my work. I specialize in collecting, organizing, and analyzing complex data sets using tools like SQL, R and Python to identify trends and make data-driven decisions. I am passionate about using data to solve problems and drive results.
